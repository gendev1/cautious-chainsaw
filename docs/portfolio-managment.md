# Portfolio Management

## Overview
Full-spectrum portfolio management tools supporting manual trading, automated rebalancing, and outsourced investment management. Built for the full range of advising styles — from hands-on trading to fully outsourced investment management.

## Features

### Trading

#### Manual Trading
- Commission-free trading of fractional shares of stocks and ETFs
- Available via web and mobile devices
- $0 commission on all trades placed through the platform

#### Automated Rebalancing
- Rules-based portfolio rebalancing engine
- Review-and-release workflow: preview proposed trades, make adjustments, and approve before execution
- Keeps clients invested, risk-aligned, and on-track
- Automation runs on autopilot between review cycles

#### Custom Models & UMAs
- Build fully custom investment models from scratch
- Create unified managed accounts (UMAs) by mixing and matching models
- Apply models across client accounts at scale

---

### Portfolio Construction

#### Personalized Indexing
- Exclude specific sectors, industries, or individual securities
- Apply values-based screens powered by Smartleaf and MSCI
- Real-time preview of how exclusions and screens affect the portfolio
- No additional cost beyond standard platform fees

#### Model Marketplace
- Access to 500+ professionally managed models
- Pricing up to 3x cheaper than the average TAMP (avg TAMP: 0.45–0.50 bps)
- Some models available starting at 0 bps
- Alternative to traditional turnkey asset management programs

#### Digital Fixed Income
- Screen fixed income securities with advanced filtering
- Click-to-trade execution
- Ongoing monitoring of fixed income holdings
- Full transparency on pricing at industry-leading rates

---

### Tax Management

#### Tax-Loss Harvesting
- Daily automated scanning for tax-loss harvesting opportunities across client portfolios
- Identifies positions with unrealized losses eligible for harvesting

#### Tax-Sensitive Rebalancing
- Rebalancing engine factors in tax implications before proposing trades
- Minimizes unnecessary capital gains when rebalancing portfolios

---

### Cash & Lending

#### High-Yield Cash Account
- No account minimums required
- Same-day transfers between cash and investment accounts
- Variable APY (subject to change)

#### Securities-Based Lines of Credit (SBLOC)
- Low-interest rate lending against existing securities
- No need to liquidate holdings or alter investment strategies
- Does not trigger capital gains events
- No setup fees or draw fees (other fees may apply per Altruist Financial LLC Fee Schedule)
- Flexible repayment terms

---

### Pricing

#### Commission-Free Trading
- $0 commissions on trades placed via web or mobile
- Applies to stocks and ETFs including fractional shares

#### Transparent Fee Structure
- Fees designed to be simple and transparent
- Aims to match or beat the most competitive legacy custodian pricing
- Full fee schedule published and publicly available
