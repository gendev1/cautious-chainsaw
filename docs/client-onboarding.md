# Client Onboarding

## Overview
Digital-first client onboarding platform that replaces paperwork-heavy processes with a consolidated, single-session workflow. What used to take days now takes minutes.

## Features

### Account Creation

#### Digital Account Opening
- Fully paperwork-free account creation process
- Accounts can be opened in under 5 minutes (including beneficiaries and trusted contacts)
- Replaces the traditional multi-day, document-heavy onboarding cycle

#### 30+ Account Types
- Supports over 30 unique account types
- Includes individual, joint, IRA, trust, and other account structures
- All account types use the same streamlined digital workflow

#### Batch Account Opening
- Open multiple accounts for a client in a single session
- No need to re-enter shared client information across accounts
- Reduces repetitive data entry for advisors managing multi-account households

#### Beneficiaries & Trusted Contacts
- Add beneficiaries and trusted contacts inline during account setup
- No separate forms or follow-up steps required
- Configured as part of the same account opening flow

---

### Account Funding & Transfers

#### Digital ACATs
- Initiate asset transfers from other institutions electronically
- No need to obtain or upload statements from the prior custodian
- Transfers complete in minutes instead of days
- Eliminates paper-based ACAT processes entirely

#### Bank Account Linking
- Clients connect and verify their bank accounts directly through the platform
- Secure verification process integrated into the onboarding flow
- Enables immediate funding once linked

#### Digital Money Movement Authorization
- All verification and authorization handled digitally — no physical documents
- Fast, secure authorization within the same onboarding session
- Clients can authorize recurring contributions and withdrawals during setup

---

### Workflow

#### Single-Session Onboarding
- Complete the entire onboarding process (open accounts, fund, authorize) in one sitting
- No back-and-forth between advisor and client after the initial session
- Designed to minimize time-to-first-investment

#### Consolidated Workflow
- All onboarding steps (account creation, funding, authorization, beneficiaries) unified in a single interface
- No switching between multiple systems or portals
- Advisor and client experience is contained within one continuous flow
