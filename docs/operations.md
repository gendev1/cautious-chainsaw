# Operations

## Overview
Administrative and operational tools to automate workflows, manage billing, generate reports, and integrate with external systems. Engineered to eliminate paperwork and free up advisor time for client relationships.

## Features

### Transitions

#### Digital ACATs
- Initiate transfers from other institutions with fully digital ACAT requests
- No paperwork or physical statements required from the prior custodian
- Significantly reduces transfer processing time

#### Bulk Transfers
- Move an entire book of business from another custodian in one coordinated effort
- Dedicated transition team assists with planning and execution
- Handles the logistics of migrating multiple client accounts simultaneously

#### Training Resources
- On-demand training library available throughout the transition process
- Deep resources covering platform features, workflows, and best practices
- Self-serve access so advisors can learn at their own pace and gain clarity at any stage

---

### Reporting

#### Built-In Reporting
- Generate complete client performance reports in a few clicks
- Replaces costly third-party reporting tools as a built-in platform feature
- Reports are designed to be client-ready and presentation-quality

#### Dynamic Reports
- Interactive, digestible views of client portfolio performance
- Clients and advisors can explore data visually rather than reading static PDFs
- Builds client trust through transparent, easy-to-understand reporting

---

### Fee Billing

#### Automated Billing
- Automate fee calculation and collection on a recurring schedule
- Reduces manual billing effort and human error
- Runs within the platform — no external billing software needed

#### Customizable Billing
- Flexible billing terms and configurations to match each advisor's fee structure
- Supports different fee schedules per client or account type
- Advisors can set terms that work for both their business and their clients

#### 360-Degree Billing
- Comprehensive in-house billing suite covering the full billing lifecycle
- Handles fee calculation, invoicing, and collection in one place
- Eliminates the need for separate third-party billing tools

---

### Integrations

#### Tech Stack Integration
- Securely connect existing advisor tools (CRM, financial planning, etc.) to the platform
- Simplifies workflows by consolidating data flows between systems
- Supports a wide range of third-party integrations

#### Full Account Visibility
- Unified dashboard view across all client accounts
- See balances, holdings, and activity in one place regardless of integration source
- Reduces context-switching between multiple portals

---

### Support

#### Dedicated Support Team
- 96% customer satisfaction rate
- Average response time under 60 seconds
- No call centers or automated phone trees — real human support

#### Advisor Idea Portal
- Submit feature requests directly to the product team
- Upvote ideas from other advisors in the community
- Interact with the Altruist advisor community to shape the product roadmap
- Every advisor becomes a contributor to platform development

#### RIA-Only Focus
- Platform exclusively serves registered investment advisors
- Will never compete for advisor clients or build retail investor products
- All product development and support priorities aligned with RIA needs
